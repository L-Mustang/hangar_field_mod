Customize your hangar from the launcher! Features
  • Customizable weather
  • Change time of day, or let it apply your local time to the hangar!
  • Switchable vehicles
  • Unlockable camera rotation and extended zoom range
  • Removable premium vehicles
  • Removed camera ellipsoid movement for tanks
      • To install
      1. Extract the archive to the War Thunder folder
      2. Add the following line to the top of config.blk:
        3. hangarBlk:t="hangar_field_mod.blk"
          The following options are available: Custom weather Custom time of day Custom vehicles Changelog
